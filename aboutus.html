About Us

We are 3 up and coming programmers, Aleksandar Simonovski, Tayveon Holmes, and Kevin Liu creating this "AI Course Tool" website for Dan Wu to complete our COMP-4990 Project Management Course at the University of Windsor, Windsor, Ontario, Canada. Dan Wu plans to use this website as a tool to teach his graduate AI course. To further his students' knowledge in AI we used important concepts from the book "THE QUEST FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE A HISTORY OF IDEAS AND ACHIEVEMENTS" by Nils J. Nilsson Stanford University (2010) and incorporated it into this website.
